Good Password Practices

  • 🔑 Use unique passwords for different accounts. A breach on one site shouldn't compromise all your accounts.
  • 📏 Prefer passphrases over short passwords. A phrase like "correct horse battery staple" is both strong and memorable.
  • 🔐 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) when available. Even a strong password benefits from a second layer of defense.
  • 🗄️ Consider using a password manager. It securely stores your unique passwords so you only need to remember one master password.
